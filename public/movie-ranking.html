Bollywood & Me: My Reel Favourites

A few films that moved me, inspired me, and stayed with me.

3 Idiots

Why I love it:
It's more than just a college story — it questions our education system, celebrates creativity, and delivers powerful life lessons with humor and heart.

Taare Zameen Par

Why I love it:
This film touched me deeply with its portrayal of a misunderstood child and the importance of seeing the world through a child’s eyes. Pure emotion and empathy.

Gangubai Kathiawadi

Why I love it:
Gritty, bold, and empowering — Alia Bhatt’s performance and Gangubai’s fight for dignity left a lasting impact on me.

Kesari

Why I love it:
A stunning tribute to courage and sacrifice, this film reminded me of the power of honor and standing up against impossible odds.
